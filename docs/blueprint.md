# **App Name**: LegisDraft

## Core Features:

- Intelligent Intake Form: A logic-based multi-step questionnaire that dynamically routes clients through relevant sections based on their unique family and asset profiles.
- Questionnaire Logic Designer: A visual administrative interface for lawyers to build the logic flow, defining the routing rules and conditional branching for the intake process.
- AI Draft Collator: An AI-powered agent tool that processes raw questionnaire data to generate a structured first-cut of a legal Will for professional review.
- Lawyer Review Portal: A dedicated interface allowing lawyers to compare AI-generated drafts side-by-side with original client intake data for quality assurance.
- Iterative AI Refinement: A tool where the AI uses reasoning to interpret specific amendments proposed by the lawyer and update the draft document precisely.
- Secure Case Vault: A secure repository for managing sensitive client responses, document versioning, and professional audit logs throughout the lifecycle.
- Client Signature Portal: A specialized execution interface for clients to review the lawyer-vetted document and provide final digital approval or signature.
- AI Monitoring Dashboard: Real-time status tracking for AI extraction tasks, identifying cases ready for human review or requiring manual intervention.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Burnt Umber (#8A5100), reflecting the traditional trust and professional gravity of the legal industry.
- Background: Soft Neutral Slate (#F7F9FC), providing a clean and non-distracting canvas for reading legal text.
- Accent: Saturated Amber (#F2994A), used for call-to-action buttons and critical progress indicators.
- Headlines are set in 'Work Sans' for a modern, authoritative feel; body copy uses 'Inter' for maximum digital legibility.
- Clean, outlined Material Symbols are used to guide users without distracting from complex legal content.
- Focused Flow Container: All questionnaire and review tasks are centralized in a 640px wide column to mimic standard document formats.
- Subtle scanning effects and status pulses communicate background AI processing activity to legal staff.
